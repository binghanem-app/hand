## Description
```
Hand is a clean, fast score keeper for the card game Hand — no more paper and pen.

Set up your table in seconds and let the app do the math while you play.

FEATURES
• Score up to 4 players, round by round
• Built-in hand types — Normal, Joker, Color, Color Joker, Super, Super Joker — each with win, loser, and victim values
• Fully customizable scoring: change every value and the number of rounds to match how your group plays
• Round-by-round history so you can see exactly how the game unfolded
• One-tap Undo for any round
• Quick manual adjustments when you need to tweak a score
• Automatic winner and final standings at the end of the game
• Your game is saved automatically — close the app and pick up right where you left off

BUILT FOR EVERYONE AT THE TABLE
• Full English and Arabic support, with right-to-left layout
• Works completely offline
• No ads, no sign-up, no accounts
• No data collected — everything stays on your device

Just open it, add your players, and play.
```

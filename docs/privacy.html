Privacy Policy · Last updated 24 June 2026

Hand — Score Keeper ("the app") is a score-keeping tool for the card game Hand. Your privacy is simple to explain: the app does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data.

Data we collect

None. The app has no user accounts, no sign-in, no analytics, no advertising, and no tracking of any kind.

Where your data lives

Everything you enter — player names, scores, round history, and settings — is stored only on your own device, in the app's local storage. It never leaves your phone and is never sent to us or to any third party. The app does not require an internet connection to function.

Third parties

The app uses no third-party SDKs, no external servers, and no payment processing. No data is shared with anyone.

Deleting your data

Because all data is stored locally, deleting the app from your device permanently removes all of its data. There is nothing stored anywhere else.

Children's privacy

The app collects no personal information from anyone, including children.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted on this page with a new "last updated" date.

Contact
